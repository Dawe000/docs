It gives you a unified set of APIs, SDKs, and integrations so you can connect your services, automate workflows, and ship production-ready applications — without spending weeks wiring together infrastructure from scratch.

## What you can do with ag0

- **Connect anything** — integrate with third-party services and internal systems using a library of pre-built connectors and a flexible REST API.
- **Automate workflows** — define triggers, conditions, and actions to automate repetitive tasks across your stack.
- **Deploy with confidence** — use built-in versioning, rollback controls, and environment management to ship safely to production.
- **Observe and debug** — get real-time logs, structured traces, and alerting so you can diagnose issues before they affect users.
- **Scale on demand** — ag0's infrastructure scales automatically with your workload, so you never have to provision capacity ahead of time.
